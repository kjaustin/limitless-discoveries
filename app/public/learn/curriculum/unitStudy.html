Unit Study

  • SCHEDULE
  • MATERIALS
  • RESOURCES


Unit studies allow us to pursue in depth investigation on a specific topic while still learning essential disciplines.

It often uses a very hands-on approach where students extensively study one topic or unit.

The Challenges and Benefits

PROS
  • Unit studies are interactive and hands-on, making this a great option for incorporating various learning styles.

  • You can go where your child’s (or your own!) interests take you, assured that your child is learning even while pursuing an interest.

  • Because information can be found online, at the library, or even in your own backyard, this approach provides a very inexpensive homeschooling option.

  • Once a topic is exhausted or interest wanes, you can move on, allowing anywhere from a few days to an entire year to be consumed by a unit study.
CHALLENGES
  • Unit studies are planning intensive. Some pre-assembled unit studies are available for purchase, sometimes even for free, but these only cover short-term needs. Families desiring to make unit studies the primary curriculum option will need to have the time to put into preparing each unit study.

  • Some subjects can be difficult to incorporate fully. For instance, while language arts and math can be tied into many unit studies, it is easy to miss important concepts and skills if a targeted curriculum is not used for those subjects.

One unit could combine multiple subjects thus providing children a more logical and natural way to learn.

Unit Study Principles

1. IN-DEPTH STUDIES

Unit studies allow children to stay with the same subject matter until all questions have been answered and all curiosity is satisfied.

2. INVESTIGATION

Unit studies provide an opportunity to encourage individual investigation. Children are more likely to want to participate in finding their own answers when it pertains to a topic of their interest.

3. APPLICATION OF THE LEARNING TOOLS

Skills are usually best developed in a systematic order. However, writing and reading find new life when driven by interest and investigation.

4. FAMILY TIME

Assignments can easily be fine-tuned to fit each child’s level while everyone studies the same topic.

The natural versatility and flexibility of a unit study will lend to many educational ideas..
