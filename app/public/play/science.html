Crystal Seashells

crystallize /ˈkristəˌlīz/: form or cause to form crystals

1. Materials You Will Need:

Borax Powder, Water, Measuring Cups and Tablespoon, Spoon, Mason Jars or Glass Containers, Seashells

2. Mix Your Saturated Solution

Boil the water. Add 3-4 Tablespoons of borax powder per 1 cup of water. I would make a 3 cup solution to get started if you are going to do several seashells. When you are mixing the solution, you will still see a tiny bit of the powder floating around and settling to the bottom. That means it’s saturated!

3. Place your seashells in glass containers

Add the solution to the glass container with your seashells and make sure to completely cover the shells. Now watch as they crystallize.

Chemistry Magic Milk

chemistry /ˈkeməstrē/: the branch of science that deals with the identification of the substances of which matter is composed

1. Gather Supplies:

Full Fat Milk, Food Coloring, Dawn Dish Soap, Cotton Swabs

2. Start by pouring your milk into a baking dish or other flat bottom surface. You don’t need a lot of milk just to cover the bottom.
3. Fill the top of the milk with drops of color. Mix them all up!
4. Put the cotton swab into dish soap and apply to the colored milk. What happens?
